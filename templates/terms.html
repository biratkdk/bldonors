WHAT DO WE DO

  1. 1. Web application to register for blood donation and track blood supply chain history

  2. 2. Create a harmonized nationwide donor database

  3. 3. Develop a stock inventory of blood collection, supply and wastage


WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  1. 1. When visiting our site – https://www.bldonors.com
    • Non-Personal Information:

      track, collect information but not limited to IP address, URLs from which people arrived to our website, operating system used, browser, page visited in our website, links clicked

    • Personal Information:

    • Personal Information:

About Us

Bldonors is an innovative approach to address global health. We provide immediate access to blood donors all over Nepal, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week! Bldonors is one of several community organizations working together as a network that respond to disasters or emergency situations in an efficient manner.
